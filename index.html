Why these tools exist

Most of what an attacker learns about your organization is already public. Certificate transparency logs, DNS records, the CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog, and dozens of other free authoritative sources tell the whole story — if you know how to read them.

Owlglass is a focused set of lenses for reading that public data. Each tool does one thing, presents the answer plainly, and links you to the sources.
