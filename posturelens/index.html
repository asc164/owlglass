How the score works

Email authentication

SPF, DKIM, DMARC, and MTA-STS are checked to confirm your domain cannot easily be spoofed and that inbound mail is encrypted in transit.

DNS hygiene

DNSSEC, CAA, and nameserver resilience reduce the risk of forged DNS responses, unauthorized certificate issuance, and provider outages.

Web security

HTTPS reachability on apex and www hostnames, plus HSTS preload status, ensure users connect securely from the first request onward.

Attack surface

Subdomains discovered through Certificate Transparency are reviewed for sensitive names and signs of dangling DNS that enable subdomain takeover.
