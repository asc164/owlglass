How PostureLens scores

Each domain receives a composite grade based on four sub-scores: Email Authentication (35%), Attack Surface (30%), DNS Hygiene (20%), and Web Security (15%). The overall grade is capped at one level above the worst sub-score — a domain failing email authentication cannot score above a D regardless of other strengths. Subdomain takeover checks run on demand via the Deep scan button per domain.
