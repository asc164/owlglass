Portfolio overview

What SurfaceLens checks

Read this if you want to understand what each finding means and where the data comes from.

Subdomain discovery

We query the public CertSpotter Certificate Transparency API for every TLS certificate ever issued to the domain.

DNS resolution

Each discovered subdomain is resolved through Google DNS-over-HTTPS for current A and AAAA records.

Open ports & CVEs

Every unique IP is looked up in Shodan InternetDB. Data is passive — we never scan you or your assets directly.

Risk scoring & grade

Each scope earns a posture grade (A–F) from weighted penalties on critical, high, and medium findings.

What's working

We highlight controls that passed: full CT enumeration, complete DNS resolution, zero CVEs, no exposed databases, no risky tags.

Limitations

Browser-only means no port scanning, banner grabbing, or screenshots. Shodan InternetDB data may be days to weeks old.
