SurfaceLens

External attack‑surface discovery — subdomains, exposed services, CVEs, and posture grading.

How SurfaceLens works

Subdomain discovery

Queries Certificate Transparency logs via CertSpotter to find hosts that have ever had a TLS certificate.

DNS resolution

Resolves each subdomain through Google DNS‑over‑HTTPS to determine which are still active.

Exposed services & CVEs

For each unique IP we query Shodan InternetDB for open ports, vulnerabilities, and tags.

Risk scoring

Hosts are scored on CVE severity, exposed databases, remote‑access ports, sensitive names, and risky service tags.

What's working

Passed checks (clean enumeration, no exposed DBs, zero CVEs, etc.) are highlighted alongside findings.

Limitations

Passive sources only. No active scanning, no authentication, no PII collection. Cached findings may lag real state by hours.
